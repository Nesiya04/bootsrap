FANTASTIC FOUR

The Fantastic Four are a team of superheroes with unique abilities gained from cosmic radiation exposure during a space mission. Reed Richards, the elastic "Mr. Fantastic"; Sue Storm, who can become invisible and project force fields, "Invisible Woman"; Johnny Storm, the flaming "Human Torch"; and Ben Grimm, the rocky "Thing", must learn to work together to master their powers and protect Earth from various threats, including their nemesis, Doctor Doom.
