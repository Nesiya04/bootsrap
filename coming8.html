BLACK WIDOW

In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Black Widow, also known as Natasha Romanoff, is a former KGB operative and a founding member of the Avengers. She is a highly skilled spy, martial artist, and assassin. Her story involves her past in the Red Room, a Soviet program that trained young girls to be assassins, and her eventual defection to S.H.I.E.L.D. The 2021 film "Black Widow" explores her past and confronts a dangerous conspiracy tied to her history.
